About us

Drum Coffee Roasters is a small and dedicated Speciality Coffee Roastery, based in

the suburbs of Mumbai. We are a small team, which is dedicated to producing high-

quality coffee. Roasted, ground, and packaged in-house, we give every bean the care

and attention it deserves before landing in your cup

Our Coffee

We hand roast our coffee beans in small batches with the state of art Giesen roaster

for high quality, freshness and consistent great taste.

Consultancy

Roasting coffee is our passion and that is what we do best. But, besides supplying

delicious coffee, we offer a full suite of support for your business – barista training,

menu development, equipment consultancy & hospitality know how..

If you wish to talk to us, drop us an email, fill out the form, or just give us a call. Let’s
